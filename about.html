About Phi Sigma Kappa

Founded on the values of brotherhood, leadership, and service, Phi Sigma Kappa has made a lasting impact at NJIT and beyond.

Our Mission

At Phi Sigma Kappa, we believe in showing up—not just for each other, but for ourselves. Whether it’s stepping up as a leader, grinding out late nights in the library, or just being someone your brothers can count on—we’re all about personal growth. We’re here to build better men, together.

Greek Life at NJIT

Greek life at NJIT is tight-knit, competitive, and full of opportunities—and Phi Sig stands out. We consistently hold the highest cumulative GPA among fraternities and are one of the highest-rated chapters by the university. We’re active, involved, and respected across campus—and we’re always raising the bar.

Fraternity History

Phi Sigma Kappa officially chartered its NJIT chapter on March 3rd, 2012—but our roots on campus go way deeper. Dating back to the 1950s, we’ve been part of NJIT’s Greek life in various forms, including local and national organizations that came before us. Today, we carry on that legacy stronger than ever.
