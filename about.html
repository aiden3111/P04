About Phi Sigma Kappa

Founded on the values of brotherhood, leadership, and service, Phi Sigma Kappa has made a lasting impact at NJIT and beyond.

Our Mission

We aim to foster a supportive environment for academic excellence, personal growth, and community involvement. Through our programs and events, we help members develop skills that serve them throughout college and life.

Greek Life at NJIT

Greek life is a cornerstone of the NJIT campus experience. Phi Sigma Kappa builds strong connections between students, provides leadership opportunities, and contributes meaningfully to campus events and philanthropy.

Fraternity History

With a rich tradition of excellence, our chapter has stood for decades as a symbol of unity and ambition. Many of our alumni remain involved, mentoring the next generation of leaders.
